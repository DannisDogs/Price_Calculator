Frequently Asked Questions

24-hour boarding means your pet stays overnight in our home environment. For example, if you drop off at 3pm and pick up at 3pm the next day, that's one 24-hour period at $55. Any extra time is charged at $5 per hour.
Your pet gets meals, walks, playtime, companionship, a comfortable sleeping area, and regular updates with photos. We treat every pet like a member of our family.
The first pet pays full price, and each additional pet adds 20% to the base cost. For example, if one pet costs $55, two pets would cost $66 total ($55 + $11 for the second pet).
